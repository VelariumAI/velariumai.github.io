VRM-AI

A verifier-centered symbolic AI system for trustworthy knowledge, reasoning, and automation.

VRM-AI is designed to solve one of AI's core problems: systems that sound correct but cannot prove what they know.

Instead of relying on probabilistic guesses, VRM-AI uses structured knowledge, verification, provenance, and controlled inference to produce answers that can be checked, audited, and trusted.

Key ideas:

  • Structured knowledge packs
  • Deterministic reasoning
  • Verification before confidence
  • Provenance and trust tiers
  • Controlled knowledge ingestion
  • No hallucination by design

Note: VRM-AI is not an LLM wrapper. It is a symbolic verification engine intended to serve as a reliable core for future AI systems.
