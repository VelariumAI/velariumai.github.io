Correctness Models (CMs)

VCSE (Verifier-Centered Symbolic Engine) is the verification and reasoning engine for Correctness Models (CMs).

Correctness Models address a core reliability problem: systems that sound correct but cannot prove what they know.

  • Structured knowledge
  • Verification before confidence
  • Provenance
  • Deterministic reasoning
  • Controlled inference
  • Not an LLM wrapper

Hosted under the VRM-AI organization for development infrastructure.
